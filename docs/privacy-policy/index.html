Hoogii's Privacy Policy

Welcome to Hoogii, a web3 wallet for the Chia network. At Hoogii, we take your privacy seriously.

This Privacy Policy describes how Hoogii collects and uses information from our Site visitors or Service users. By visiting the Site and/or using the Services, you acknowledge and accept the terms described in this Policy. Please note that this Policy does not apply to information collected through third-party websites or services or include those that you submit to us through emails, text messages or other electronic messages or offline.

Hoogii will never ask you to share your private keys or wallet seed. NEVER trust anyone or any site that asks you to enter your private keys or wallet seed.

Information Collection​

Information that Hoogii collects users may include:

  1. The list of web pages a user has visited, as well as associated data. For example: page title, time of visit…etc
  2. Network information regarding transactions, clicks, mouse position, scroll, or keystroke logging
  3. Location information including IP address and information about the user’s device

Information Use​

Hoogii’s primary purpose in collecting information is to help our team improve our Services. We will not disclose your information to other parties or sell your Personal Information to anyone.

Policy Updates​

Hoogii will notify users of changes to this policy by updating the last updated date at the bottom of this page. If you do not accept the revised policies, please immediately stop using any service of Hoogii.

Contact Hoogii​

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at: contact@hoogii.app
